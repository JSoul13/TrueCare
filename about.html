Our Mission

Trusted Treatment Connect is dedicated to eliminating slimy brokers in the drug, alcohol, and mental health treatment industry. We connect patients with verified insurance to reputable facilities, ensuring a transparent, scam-free process. Our platform provides facilities with high-quality, qualified leads, helping patients find the care they need.
